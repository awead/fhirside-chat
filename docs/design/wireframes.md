- **Border radius:** 8px
- **Margin between:** 8px vertical

- **Height:** 48px (mobile: 44px min)
- **Padding:** 12px horizontal
- **Border radius:** 6px
- **Border:** 1px solid gray-300
- **Focus:** 2px ring, border blue-500

- **Height:** 64px (mobile: 56px)
- **Padding:** 16px horizontal
- **Shadow:** Light bottom shadow
- **Background:** White
